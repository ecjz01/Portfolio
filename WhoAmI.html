Who Am I?

From the Keirsey personality quiz I found out that I am of the category Guardian (45% of the population) and from the Humanmetrics Jung Typology Test I am an ISTJ (Introvert 31%, Sensing 16%, Thinking 66%, and Judging 19%). This means that I am of the group Inspectors within the Guardian personality.

From the Keirsey quiz I agree with a few of the characteristics of an Inspector which include persevering and following the rules. This is because I usually get very annoyed from not being able to complete or finish something and I will do as much as possible to get something done by a deadline. However, I only somewhat agree with patience, because over the years I think that I have gotten more patient, however I am still impatient for certain things that I am very excited for.

I agree with many of the characteristics of an ISTJ, including that I am punctual and I am well aware of my areas of interest and/or responsibility (The reason it is called inspector). This is because I pride myself most of the time on being punctual when arriving to events/meetups and when going out, one of the things I plan the most is arriving on time no matter what. I agree with the awareness of my areas of interest andéor responsibility to a degree, because I am currently aware of general areas that I want to avoid/involve myself in, such as I would prefer to do something STEM related and hopefully avoid something related to caregiving or health.

Another thing that ISTJ’s prefer, is when working ISTJ’s prefer having just facts given to them and are most productive when they have a step-by-step plan. I agree with this, because I prefer to have facts rather than opinions when working or speaking with others. Also, I tend to do better and work faster on projects that involve less creative thinking and more step-by-step planning, whether I am given a plan to do or I have to come up with a plan for myself.

The final thing that the quiz has told me is that ISTJ’s are easily frustrated by others inconsistencies especially when others don’t keep their commitments, but usually keep their feelings to themselves. I agree with the first part somewhat, because when someone doesn’t do their part on a project or cancels last minute on plans I get somewhat irritated but not really, because I have gotten used to changing expectations closer to reality between different people. However, I especially agree with the final [art of usually keeping their feelings to themselves, because if I’m annoyed or angry with someone I always try my best not to show it with anyone.

Some of the career choice areas for ISTJ include Management, Technical and Health Care. I agree with the first two, because before taking this test, I was already aware that I wanted to do something like accounting or computer science (which fall under management and technical). However, I disagree with Health Care, because my social skills are not good enough to help others, like you would need to in Health Care.

Overall, I found the quizzes quite accurate when it came to characteristics and tendencies that my personality has. Along with being even more accurate if you average out the information from both quizzes to get rid of the things that may not be as accurate.
